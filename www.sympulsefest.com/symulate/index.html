The Premier Business Festival

Symulate attracts the best brains from all over the country and gives them a common platform to compete and demonstrate their ability in events ranging from Finance, Best Manager, Best Entrepreneur to Business Quest. The events span over four days where every participant and college contingent battles it out for the top spot. Symulate begins with the ‘Academic Summit’, wherein eminent personalities from different fields such as corporate, sports, media and defence come together and enlighten students. This is followed by various events where business takes a new and exciting form and participants can test their mettle against each other.
Eligibility Criteria: Must be an Under Graduate college student.
