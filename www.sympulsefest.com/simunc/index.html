About SIMUNC

Symbiosis International Model United Nations is an initiative to make students global leaders of tomorrow. Through this conference, we hope to provide a unique opportunity to engage students to be more productive with their potential while developing strong friendships.
The conference aims to draw attention to the fundamental issues of global importance through different committees. The focus will be on providing them with the ability to pause and think of concrete solutions and ways which in the future would help heal the world. A new era of revolution isn't far if we push the students beyond what they feel they can do and make them believe that they have the ability to achieve what they envision. From Anne Frank, leaving a mark at 14 to Malala creating an impact on the world at 16. We only realize that we don't need to grow up to find greatness. One is already so much more than what they think they are. Our team has put their best efforts into planning challenging topics as well as spirited social occasions for the participating students to enjoy getting to know each other.

Letter from the Co-Presidents

Greetings,

It gives us immense pleasure to welcome you to the Symbiosis International Model United Nations Conference '19. We are gratified to be heading the 10th edition of this prestigious conference. This three-day expedition will give you memories of a lifetime and take you on the most intense journey ever. At SIMUNC you can either delve into the past and recreate the entire history, create peace and mutual understanding in your time, use your influence to wreak havoc or anarchy among nations or you can simply use your negotiation skills to barter secrets and strategies to put your nation on a higher pedestal.

This year, we bring to you 4 carefully curated committees which are going to focus on varied aspects affecting the international community as a whole. The General Assembly I, a double delegation committee focuses on resolving hostility between nations with regards to the South China Sea and delegates face the responsibility of preventing a major war from breaking out. The Historic Security Council takes us back to a milestone era for not only the subcontinent but the whole world, where the superpowers of the world are in a nuclear standoff and anything could spark the world war 3. Delegates get to change the course of history, and make the world a better place. The Transfer Policy Meet aims to bring together debate and football in a fusion which will see participants holding key portfolios in the footballing fraternity and shape the future of the biggest football leagues in the world. The Global Compact brings to you the financial crisis of 2008, where the world economy is collapsing and the markets are crashing, it is up to the delegates to save the world economy.

We look forward to hosting you from the 18th to the 20th of January, 2019.

Devansh Shah - Co-President


Vishal Shivakumar - Co-President
